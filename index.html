Our Recycling Process

How Solar Panel Recycling Works

Our advanced 8-step recycling process ensures maximum material recovery while maintaining the highest environmental standards

1

Collection & Transport

We collect end-of-life solar panels from your location using specialized vehicles

  • Free pickup service
  • Safe transportation
  • Tracking system
2

Inspection & Testing

Each panel is inspected and tested to determine the best recycling approach

  • Quality assessment
  • Component analysis
  • Data logging
3

Dismantling

Careful disassembly of frames, junction boxes and protective layers

  • Manual separation
  • Frame removal
  • Component sorting
4

Chemical Treatment

Safe extraction and separation of silicon cells from glass and other materials

  • Eco-friendly solvents
  • Cell extraction
  • Purity enhancement
5

Thermal Processing

High-temperature treatment to recover valuable metals and materials

  • Controlled heating
  • Metal recovery
  • Energy capture
6

Material Separation

Advanced separation techniques to isolate glass, silicon, silver, and copper

  • Mechanical sorting
  • 95% recovery rate
  • Quality control
7

Purification

Recovered materials are purified to meet industry standards for reuse

  • Chemical refinement
  • Quality testing
  • Certification
8

Redistribution

Purified materials are packaged and sent to manufacturers for new products

  • New solar panels
  • Electronic components
  • Building materials

Why Solar Recycling Matters

Every solar panel recycled is a step towards a sustainable future. Here's how we're making a difference for our planet.

Reduce Landfill Waste

Every solar panel recycled keeps e-toxic and hazardous materials out of landfills and groundwater from contamination.

90% less waste

Save Natural Resources

Recovering valuable materials like silicon, copper, and glass reduces mining and preserves natural resources.

95% recovery rate

Prevent Toxic Pollution

Safe handling of hazardous materials limits the lead and cadmium contamination and health hazards.

100% safe disposal

Circular Economy

Transform waste into resources, creating a sustainable cycle where materials are continuously reused and repurposed.

Zero to landfill

Lower Carbon Footprint

Recycling solar panels uses significantly less energy than producing new materials from raw resources.

75% less energy

Water Conservation

Reduces water consumption in manufacturing processes by reusing recovered materials instead of extraction.

Millions of liters saved
